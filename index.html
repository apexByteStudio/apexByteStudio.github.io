Privacy Policy for NoteVault

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the NoteVault mobile application ("NoteVault", "the app", "we", "us") handles your information. NoteVault is developed by AppLab Ltd. By installing or using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

NoteVault is designed to keep your data on your device. We collect and process the following:

  • Your notes and content: The notes, folders, formatting, reminders, and any content you create are stored locally on your device in the app's database. We do not transmit or read your notes on our servers — we do not operate any servers.
  • Google Drive backup data (optional): If you choose to back up or restore, the app stores a copy of your local database file in your own Google Drive account. Only you can access it; we cannot.
  • Advertising data: We display ads using Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID), IP address, and usage data to serve and measure ads. See Section 4.
  • Biometric data: Secured notes use your device's biometric system (fingerprint/face). This authentication is handled entirely by Android — we never receive, store, or transmit your biometric data.

2. How We Use Information

  • To store, display, and manage the notes and reminders you create.
  • To back up and restore your data to/from your own Google Drive, when you request it.
  • To display advertisements that help keep the app free.
  • To send local reminder notifications you schedule.

3. Device Permissions

NoteVault requests these permissions for the stated purposes only:

  • Internet / Network state: to serve ads and to back up/restore via Google Drive.
  • Notifications: to show reminders you set.
  • Schedule exact alarms: to fire reminders at the precise time you choose.
  • Boot completed: to reschedule your reminders after the device restarts.
  • Google accounts: to let you select the Google account for Drive backup.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — https://policies.google.com/privacy. You can learn about and control ad personalization at https://adssettings.google.com.
  • Google Drive API / Google Play Services (optional backup) — https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your notes live on your device until you delete them. Deleted notes go to Trash and are automatically purged after 30 days.
  • To remove all local data, uninstall the app or clear its storage in Android settings.
  • To remove your cloud backup, delete the backup file from your Google Drive.

6. Children's Privacy

NoteVault is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because your content stays on your device and in your own Google Drive, you remain in direct control of your data. Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data, and to opt out of personalized ads via your device's ad settings or the in-app consent prompt.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
